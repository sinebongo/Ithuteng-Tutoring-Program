ABOUT OUR JOURNEY

Welcome to our tutoring company! We are a team of dedicated educators and young professionals who are committed to helping students achieve their full potential. Our company was founded in 2019 by two friends, one with a background in computer science and Mathematics, and the other with a background in chemistry and chemical engineering.

Both of our founders were top students in their high school, ranking in the top 5 of their class. They understand the importance of a solid education and the impact it can have on a person's future. With this in mind, they decided to start their own tutoring company to provide students with the personalized attention and support they need to succeed.

Our team is made up of experienced educators who specialize in a variety of subjects. We offer tutoring for grade 10, 11 and 12, from high school to those upgrading their mark. Our subjects include math, science, Accounting, Life Sciences, and more. We believe that every student is unique, and we tailor our teaching approach to meet the individual needs of each student.

We understand that not all students learn at the same pace or in the same way, which is why encourage authenticity in group tutoring. Our flexible scheduling options allow students to receive the help they need, when they need it.

At our tutoring company, we are committed to helping students achieve their full potential. We believe that with the right guidance and support, any student can reach their goals. Thank you for considering us for your tutoring needs.
