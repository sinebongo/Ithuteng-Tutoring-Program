About ITP

ITP is a team of young passionate, dedicated, hardworking, and reliable tutors who place learning above everything else. The program was founded in 2019 as response to the need for traditional and non-traditional learning that promotes self-discovery, knowledge construction, group-based discussions, transformation and independent learning, which is beneficial to learners who want to exercise their flexibility and options on what to learn.
